Our team was using Scrum framework to divide development cycle into several sprints. We used Jira Software to manage our backlog, board and tasks. Jira helped to equally distribute tasks, keep track of team's progress. Jura's integration with Github provided clean and robust repository workflow. In order to minimize possible bugs and issues, our team followed a strict pull request merge policy, which required code reviews and approvals of both other team members.

During the whole development process our team was helding daily meetings to coordinate out efforts, to discuss potential difficulties or different solutions to the problems we were facing.
